Introduction to the building

Regus serviced office at Regus - Worc@Q2 offers a premium workspace experience for businesses seeking a professional and flexible serviced office for rent in District 2. Located at 21 Vo Truong Toan, Thao Dien ward, District 2, HCMC, this strategic address is in the heart of Thao Dien’s vibrant commercial and residential hub, surrounded by international schools, high-end apartments, and lifestyle amenities. Operated by Regus – a global leader in flexible workspace solutions – the facility provides a variety of options, including private offices, coworking desks, and meeting rooms, catering to different business sizes and operational needs. Launched in a modern Grade A-standard building, the space is competitively priced from 387 USD per person per month, making it an attractive choice for both local enterprises and foreign investors. The location ensures excellent connectivity via Hanoi Highway and Mai Chi Tho Boulevard, offering quick access to Thu Thiem and central District 1. Tenants benefit from top-tier amenities such as high-speed internet, professional reception services, 24/7 secure access, and fully equipped meeting rooms. This workspace is particularly suitable for office staff, executives, HR personnel, assistants, and secretaries who value convenience, prestige, and a productive environment. With its combination of prime location, modern facilities, and Regus’s global standard of service, Regus - Worc@Q2 is a reliable choice for businesses aiming to establish or expand their presence in District 2.
